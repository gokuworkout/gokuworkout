GOKU WORKOUT TRANINING

Goku's training routine includes a variety of exercises, including:
Aerobic
Goku used to hike up the Korin Tower, so you can try adding high intensity cardio like running, hiking, or using the Stairmaster to your routine. Ankle weights
Wear ankle weights throughout the day to add an extra load on your leg muscles and force your body to adapt. Bodyweight exercises
You can try exercises like pull ups, knee to chest jump lunges, shoulder tap Hindu push ups, burpee double tuck jumps, side plank dips with leg up, and pistol squats. Boxing and jumping
You can try combining jumping and boxing in a half burpee glass boxing.
